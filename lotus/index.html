AN INSIGHT INTO LOTUS

LUXURY

We never compromise on luxury or hospitality as without the two a hotel experience is neither complete nor defined. With years of experience in providing the same, we go a step ahead with a budget-friendly tariff.

GREAT SERVICES

Exquisite facilities and services, especially for the people on the fast-lane is our métier. Ranging from complimentary breakfast, wake-up call, airport pick-up & drop to keeping you fit with a health studio and also a boardroom for all your vital conferences.

ONLINE RESERVATION

Embracing technology and the route of the 21st century, we have made life way easier with simple steps for booking your reservation unlike the old-telephone-in regime with a simple online portal. Now confirm your reservation months in advance as well as choose the suite you be happy in.

For Extended Stays at Lotus

The forte of an apartment hotel is its invaluable kitchenette facility.
