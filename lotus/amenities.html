AMENITIES AT LOTUS

Regardless of the purpose of your trip, find a range of amenities to suit your needs at our hotel apartments in Chennai. From holding conference calls, to getting your heart rate pumping in the fitness corner, we have the services to make your trip a hassle-free one.

THE HUB OF CHENNAI

Located in the centre of Chennai and all its major commercial and corporate attractions, anything and everything is now just within a few miles from your favourite hotel and apartment hotels.

CLOSE TO THE SEA

Situated just 2 miles from the second longest beach in the world, Marina Beach, Lotus is the ideal option for a long walk down history as well as loaded with fun, games and activities for people across all age groups.

HEALTH STUDIO

We at Lotus are staunch believers that an active human is a healthy human. Therefore, we have with us a modest health studio so that you may keep yourself fit even when you are away from your home or your gym. Stay in your prime, all the time.

TAXI SERVICE

We provide full end taxi service for Airport Pick-up and Drop-off. Regardless of the time, we make sure that you reach where you want to be and come back when you want to.

RESIDENTS’ LOUNGE

Fitted with exquisite wall art and quirky furniture, our residents’ lounge is the ideal space to meet with friends, engage in some reading or even catch up on work.
