LOTUS VENKATARAMAN RD.

The Lotus Apartment Hotel on Venkatraman Street has tucked away in a quiet residential area, providing the privacy and serenity you and your family needs whilst being a few minutes away from the hustle bustle of T-Nagar. It is close to business and commercial hubs, corporate offices, shopping malls, cultural centres and more popular with business travellers. The apartment hotel has a large well laid out reception area, 36 modern rooms and suites.

The hotel boasts of a fitness centre with multi-gym machines, travel desk, and the lounge areas on every floor, and contemporary décor elevates the ambience. The restaurant offers buffet breakfast and also has an à-la-carte menu for lunch and dinner. The apartment hotel is also a stone’s throw away from popular restaurants – speak to any of our team members to order-in your favourite food.
