About us

The Lotus Hotels and Apartment Hotels are a chain of hotels that is synchronous with the fast-moving lifestyle of the 21st century offering luxurious facilities and hospitality. Having a lineage from the GRT Hotels and Resorts, the three properties are located at the prime centre of the city, Chennai. Being a quintessential lynchpin at providing the finest experience for a wide range of guests, including corporate and leisure travellers as well as long-stay guests. Regardless, if you are in the city on a short business trip, a leisure break or looking to accommodate various guests in light of various events such as wedding etc, we assure you that Lotus Hotels are the best there is.

The chain of apartment hotels are centrally located in the vicinity of major business sectors, embassies, corporate houses and major hospitals, yet within the serene quiet residential area away from the hustling lifestyle of T-Nagar. With the ease of access being a major forte, we sit just 30 minutes away from the airport as well as minutes away from Chennai Central and Egmore Railway Terminals. Renowned for her culture and shopping streets, T-Nagar is the commercial hub for almost everything under the sun including exquisite silk sarees, bridal wear, and gold and diamond jewellery.
