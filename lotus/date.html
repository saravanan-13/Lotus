AN INSIGHT INTO OUR

LUXURY

We never compromise on luxury or hospitality as without the two a hotel experience is not complete nor defined. With years of experience in providing the same, we go a step ahead with a budget-friendly tariff.

GREAT SERVICES

Exquisite facilities and services, especially for the people on the fast-lane is our métier. Ranging from complimentary breakfast, wake-up call, airport pick-up & drop to keeping you fit with a health studio and also a boardroom for all your vital conferences.

ONLINE RESERVATION

Embracing technology and the route of the 21st century, we have made life way easier with simple steps for rooking your reservation unlike the old-telephone-in regime with a simple online portal. Now confirm your reservation months in advance as well as choose the suite you be happy in.

Extended Stay/Long Stay

The forte of an apartment hotel is its invaluable kitchenette facility within one's own room. Installed for the people living life in the fast lane, the in-room kitchenette is a full-fledged kitchen area that is created and designed to look chic at the same time cater to all your culinary desires and midnight munchies. Prefered by our long-stay guests, it is the perfect addition to the room as because it is the most viable option against blowing money on food day-in-day-out. We want you to be in your comfort zone regardless of anything.
