The Lotus hotel- Sameera

The hotel is centrally located on Anna Salai, the most prominent and historic arterial road in the city of Chennai. The property is close to the commercial and business hubs, shopping districts, hospitals, wedding halls, cultural and entertainment centers as well as the Egmore and Central Railway Stations. Historic landmarks like the Marina Beach (second longest beach in the world), Government Museum, The Chennai Port, Fort St George and the Connemara Library are just few kilometres away. Also in close proximity are centuries-old temples, two-century-old Christ Church School and mosques.

The Lotus Sameera has 59 rooms with 9 suites have been designed with your comfort in mind and come with state-of-the-art technology, sound-proof rooms, unrestricted city views, and ease of access to central Chennai. Enter the hotel through the service lane, into the spacious driveway and proceed to check-in at the lobby on the 2nd floor. In-house guests have access to free unlimited Wi-Fi and complimentary buffet breakfast, offering a selection of Indian and International dishes. The rooms and common areas have been designed with traditional motifs that echo the culture of Chennai.
